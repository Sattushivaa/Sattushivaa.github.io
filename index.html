Welcome to the basics of online safety

We will firstly see what a online sefety is and then we will see some tips to stay safe.



What is a online safety

Online safety represents the process of staying safe on the Internet – basically making sure online security threats don’t endanger your personal information or the integrity of the device you are using.

How can we stay safe

Here are some tips that you can use to ensure more safety

  1. Keep your systems and software up-to-date.
  2. Always have current/updated anti-virus running.
  3. Avoid phishing scams.
  4. Use a complex password or a password manager.
  5. Be careful what you click. A disreputable website can link you to cybercriminials.
  6. Never leave your computer or devices unattended. Lock any screens when you head to the restroom. Any open system is an open invitation to your data.
  7. Protect your data.
  8. For all personal files, back up your data. You never know when you may lose your hard drive, and if the data will be recoverable. There are many cloud storage options, or an external drive is also an option.
  9. When shopping online, or sharing sensitive data, be sure you are sending information encrypted by looking for “https” or a lock icon in your address bar.
  10. Be smart about what you share something on social media.
  11. Be sure to monitor your financial and social media accounts for suspicious activity.
